Imagine your keyboard is a piece of paper, and construct letters using three rows of letter keys. "Draw" your letters by typing, then hit "Enter." Correct letter will display on the scree; yellow screen will appear if the letter is incorrect. Use shapes of the same letter across the keyboard.
